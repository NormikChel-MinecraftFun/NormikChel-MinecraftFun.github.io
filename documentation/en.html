Documentation

Detailed descriptions of all tools available on the site.

House Block Calculator

Calculates the number of blocks needed to build a house of given dimensions, including walls, floor, and roof.

Random Coordinates Generator

Generates random X, Y, Z coordinates within Minecraft world limits for teleportation via the /tp command.

In-Game Time Converter

Converts real-world minutes to in-game days and vice versa. 1 in-game day = 20 real minutes.

Experience Calculator

Shows how many experience levels you’ll gain from a given amount of XP points, based on Minecraft’s leveling formula.

Item Name Generator

Creates unique and creative names for items, perfect for mods, custom maps, or roleplay servers.

Creeper Farm Calculator

Calculates how many creepers you need to kill to obtain a desired amount of gunpowder (assuming 100% drop chance under normal conditions).

Random Seed Generator

Generates a random numeric seed for creating a new Minecraft world. Works for both Java and Bedrock Edition.

Armor Durability Calculator

Determines how many hits armor made of different materials (leather, gold, iron, diamond, etc.) can withstand before breaking.

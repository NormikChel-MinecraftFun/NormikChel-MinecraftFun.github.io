Documentation

Detailed descriptions of all tools available on the site.

🧱 House Block Calculator

Calculates blocks needed for a house of given dimensions, including walls, floor, and roof.

🧭 Random Coordinates Generator

Generates random X, Y, Z coordinates within Minecraft world limits for teleportation via /tp.

⏳ In-Game Time Converter

Converts real minutes to in-game days and vice versa. 1 in-game day = 20 real minutes.

✨ Experience Calculator

Shows how many experience levels you’ll gain from a given XP amount, based on Minecraft’s formula.

🏷️ Item Name Generator

Creates unique and creative names for items, perfect for mods, custom maps, or roleplay servers.

💥 Creeper Farm Calculator

Calculates how many creepers to kill for a desired amount of gunpowder (100% drop chance assumed).

🌱 Random Seed Generator

Generates a random numeric seed for a new Minecraft world. Works for Java and Bedrock Edition.

🛡️ Armor Durability Calculator

Determines how many hits armor (leather, gold, iron, diamond, etc.) can withstand before breaking.
